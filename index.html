UPTRADE

UPTRADE is one of the very few day-trading firms in Finland. UPTRADE was founded in the January of 2019. UPTRADE has specialized mainly on futures, stocks and forex markets. UPTRADE works from Tampere which is city located in Finland, 180 kilometers north from capital city Helsinki. UPTRADE has aspiring destination to grow its capital 10% every month following the 2-percents rule while working on that goal.
